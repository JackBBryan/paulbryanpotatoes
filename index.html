Paul Bryan Potatoes

Paul Bryan Potatoes is a small family run business based in the heart of the Sussex countryside.

Bryan Potatoes was originally started over 40 years ago by Paul Bryan and is now run by his son Dan Bryan.

Our business specialises in the supply of potatoes to fish and chip shops, farm shops, restaurants and pubs across the South East of England.
